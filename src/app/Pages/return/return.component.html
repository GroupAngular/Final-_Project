Return and Refund Policy

Last updated: [30-1-2025]

1. Returns

We accept returns within 15 days of delivery. To be eligible for a return, your item must be unused, in the same condition as you received it, and in its original packaging. Please ensure the item is securely packed to avoid damage during return shipping.

2. Refunds

Once your return is received and inspected, we will send you an email to notify you that we have received your returned item. We will also notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If approved, your refund will be processed, and a credit will automatically be applied to your original method of payment within 7 business days.

3. Non-Refundable Items

Certain items are non-refundable, including:

  • Custom-made or personalized furniture.
  • Items marked as "final sale" or "non-returnable" at the time of purchase.
  • Damaged items due to misuse or improper care.

4. Exchanges

We only replace items if they are defective or damaged. If you need to exchange an item, please contact us at hello@efreshit.com with your order details and the reason for the exchange.

5. Shipping for Returns

You will be responsible for paying the shipping costs for returning your item. Shipping costs are non-refundable. If you receive a refund, the cost of return shipping will be deducted from your refund.

6. Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us immediately at hello@efreshit.com. We will arrange for a replacement or refund at no additional cost to you.
